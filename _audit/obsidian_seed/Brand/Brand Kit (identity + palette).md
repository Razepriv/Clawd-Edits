## Voice

- Conversational, direct, developer-native.
- Specific numbers > vague claims (e.g. "benchmark went from 58 % to 70 %").
- Always end on a deliverable: "Comment 'X' → I send you Y."
- No hype phrases. No emojis in captions (emojis fine in DM payload).
